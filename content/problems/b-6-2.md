## Given

An $n$-vertex polygon $P$ without holes.

## Problem

Find the triangulation computed by the *plane sweep triangulation algorithm*.

## Status

Open (Atallah, Callahan, and Goodrich [2]).

## Definitions

See [PST]({{< relref "./a-9-1.md" >}}) for a description of the algorithm.

## Remarks

The problem of finding some arbitrary triangulation is in $\NC$ (Goodrich [1]). If the polygon is allowed to have holes, the problem is $\P$-complete [2]. See [PST]({{< relref "./a-9-1.md" >}}).
