## Given

Let $S$ be a finite set and $F$ be the free group generated by $S$. Let $\overline{S} = \\{s, s^{-1} \mid s \in S\\}$, where $s^{-1}$ denotes the inverse of $s$. Let $\overline{S}^*$ denote the set of all finite words over $\overline{S}$. Let $k \in \N$. Let $U = \\{u_1, \ldots, u_m\\} \subseteq \overline{S}^*$, where $m \in \N$.

## Problem

Does $\langle U \rangle$ have rank $k$? The rank is the number of elements in a minimal generating set.

## Status

$\P$-complete (Avenhaus and Madlener [1]).
