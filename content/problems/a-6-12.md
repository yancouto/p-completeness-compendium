## Given

A *well-founded relation* $(V, E)$ and $x_1, x_2 \in V$. Let $f$ be the *Mostowski epimorphism* of the relation. Refer to <a href="#ref-1" class="reference-citation">[1]</a> for definitions.

## Problem

Is $f(x_1) = f(x_2)$?

## Status

$\P$-complete (Dahlhaus <a href="#ref-1" class="reference-citation">[1]</a>).

## Remarks

The reduction to show hardness is from [MCVP]({{< relref "./a-1-3.md" >}}).
The result indicates that the programming language SETL may not be well suited to parallelism <a href="#ref-1" class="reference-citation">[1]</a>. See Problem B.3.1 for a related open problem.
