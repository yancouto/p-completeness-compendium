## Given

An undirected graph $G = (V, E)$ with an ordering on its edges and a distinguished edge $e \in E$.

## Problem

Is $e$ in the lexicographically first maximal matching of $G$?

## Definitions

A matching is *maximal* if it cannot be extended.

## Status

Open (Mayr and Subramanian [[1]](#1)).

## Remarks

LFMM is $\NC$ equivalent to [CCVP]({{< relref "./b-8-1.md" >}}) [[1]](#1). This problem resembles [LFMIS]({{< relref "./a-2-1.md" >}}) that is $\P$-complete. A $\P$-completeness proof for LFMM would imply that [EWM]({{< relref "./b-1-3.md" >}}), is also $\P$-complete.
