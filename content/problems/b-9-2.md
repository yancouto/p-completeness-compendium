## Given

A graph $G = (V, E)$ and a vertex $s$.

## Problem

Construct the depth-first search numbering of $G$ starting from vertex $s$.

## Status

Open (Aggarwal and Anderson <a href="#ref-1" class="reference-citation">[1]</a>, Aggarwal, Anderson, and Kao <a href="#ref-2" class="reference-citation">[2]</a>).

## Remarks

$\mathsf{RNC}$ algorithms are now known for both the undirected <a href="#ref-1" class="reference-citation">[1]</a> and directed <a href="#ref-2" class="reference-citation">[2]</a> cases, subsuming earlier $\mathsf{RNC}$ results for planar graphs (Smith <a href="#ref-3" class="reference-citation">[3]</a>). For directed acyclic graphs, DFS is in $\NC$ (de la Torre and Kruskal <a href="#ref-4" class="reference-citation">[4]</a><a href="#ref-5" class="reference-citation">[5]</a>, Greenlaw <a href="#ref-6" class="reference-citation">[6]</a>).
