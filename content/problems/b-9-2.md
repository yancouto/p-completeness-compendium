## Given

A graph $G = (V, E)$ and a vertex $s$.

## Problem

Construct the depth-first search numbering of $G$ starting from vertex $s$.

## Status

Open problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem B.9.2.

## Remarks

$\text{RNC}$ algorithms are now known for both the undirected [5] and directed [6] cases, subsuming earlier $\text{RNC}$ results for planar graphs (Smith [334]). For directed acyclic graphs, DFS is in $\text{NC}$ (de la Torre and Kruskal [85, 86], Greenlaw [135]).
