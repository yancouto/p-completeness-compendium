## Given

Two unrooted trees $T = (V, E)$ and $T' = (V', E')$.

## Problem

Is $T$ isomorphic to a subtree of $T'$?

## Definitions

See [BDGI]({{< relref "./b-1-1.md" >}}).


## Status

Open.

## Remarks

$\mathsf{RNC}^3$ algorithms for the problem were developed independently in [[1]](#1)[[2]](#2). Each uses randomization solely to solve a number of *Bipartite Matching Problems*, see [PME]({{< relref "./b-9-8.md" >}}). Lindell shows that the problem of determining whether two unrooted trees are isomorphic, namely the *Tree Isomorphism Problem*, can be solved in $\mathsf{DLOG}$; therefore, on an EREW-PRAM in $O(\log n)$ time using $n^{O(1)}$ processors [[3]](#3).
