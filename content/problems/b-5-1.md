## Given

Two $n$-bit positive integers $a$ and $b$.

## Problem

Compute integers $s$ and $t$ such that $as + bt = \gcd(a, b)$.

## Status

Open (Borodin, von zur Gathen, and Hopcroft [[1]](#1)).

## Remarks

The analogous problem for $n^\text{th}$ degree polynomials is in $\NC$ [[1]](#1). Deng shows several other problems are $\NC$-equivalent to a closely related problem [[2]](#2). Two of these are expanding the continued fraction of the ratio of two integers and computing the sequence of convergents of two integers. See also Shallcross, Pan, and Lin-Kriz [[3]](#3) and the remarks for [Opt2ILP]({{< relref "./b-2-1.md" >}}).
