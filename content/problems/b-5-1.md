## Given

Two $n$-bit positive integers $a$ and $b$.

## Problem

Compute integers $s$ and $t$ such that $as + bt = \gcd(a, b)$.

## Status

Open problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem B.5.1.

## Remarks

The analogous problem for $n$th degree polynomials is in $\text{NC}$ [43]. Deng shows several other problems are $\text{NC}$-equivalent to a closely related problem [90]. Two of these are expanding the continued fraction of the ratio of two integers and computing the sequence of convergents of two integers. See also Shallcross, Pan, and Lin-Kriz [329] and remarks following Problem B.2.1.
