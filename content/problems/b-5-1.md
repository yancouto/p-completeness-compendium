## Given

Two $n$-bit positive integers $a$ and $b$.

## Problem

Compute integers $s$ and $t$ such that $as + bt = \gcd(a, b)$.

## Status

Open (Borodin, von zur Gathen, and Hopcroft <a href="#ref-1" class="reference-citation">[1]</a>).

## Remarks

The analogous problem for $n^\text{th}$ degree polynomials is in $\NC$ <a href="#ref-1" class="reference-citation">[1]</a>. Deng shows several other problems are $\NC$-equivalent to a closely related problem <a href="#ref-2" class="reference-citation">[2]</a>. Two of these are expanding the continued fraction of the ratio of two integers and computing the sequence of convergents of two integers. See also Shallcross, Pan, and Lin-Kriz <a href="#ref-3" class="reference-citation">[3]</a> and the remarks for [Opt2ILP]({{< relref "./b-2-1.md" >}}).
