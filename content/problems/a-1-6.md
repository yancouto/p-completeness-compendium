## Given

An encoding $\alpha$ of a monotone Boolean circuit $\alpha$, inputs $x_1, \ldots, x_n$, and designated output $y$. In addition to the restrictions of Problem A.1.4, this version requires the circuit to be synchronous. That is, each level in the circuit can receive its inputs only from gates on the preceding level.

## Problem

Is output $y$ of $\alpha$ true on input $x_1, \ldots, x_n$?

## Status

P-complete problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem A.1.6.
