## Given

A monadic Krom formula $F$. A Krom formula is a $2$-CNF formula. That is, a formula in CNF with at most two disjuncts per clause. Let $\varphi$ and $\psi$ be predicate letters that may be preceded by a negation. A conjunct of $F$ is monadic if it is of the form $(\varphi(y_1) \vee \psi(y_1))$.

## Problem

Is $F$ satisfiable?

## Status

P-complete problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem A.6.4.
