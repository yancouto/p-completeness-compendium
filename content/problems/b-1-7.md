## Given

An undirected, planar, bipartite graph $G = (V, E)$ with a numbering on the vertices in $V$.

## Problem

Find the lexicographically first maximal independent set.

## Status

Open (Miyano [1]).

## Remarks

See [LFMIS]({{< relref "./a-2-1.md" >}}). Finding the lexicographically first maximal subgraph of maximum degree one in planar, bipartite graphs of degree at most three is $\P$-complete [1].
