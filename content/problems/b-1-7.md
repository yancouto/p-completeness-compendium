## Given

An undirected, planar, bipartite graph $G = (V, E)$ with a numbering on the vertices in $V$.

## Problem

Find the lexicographically first maximal independent set.

## Status

Open problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem B.1.7.

## Remarks

See LFMIS. Finding the lexicographically first maximal subgraph of maximum degree one in planar, bipartite graphs of degree at most three is $\P$-complete [1].
