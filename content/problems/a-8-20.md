## Given

A finitely presented algebra $A = (M, \mathcal{A}, \Gamma)$ and terms $x_1, \ldots, x_n, y$. Let $[x] = \\{y \in \tau \mid x \equiv y\\}$.

## Problem

Is $[y]$ contained in the subalgebra generated by $[x_1], \ldots, [x_n]$?

## Definitions

See [UWPFPA]({{< relref "./a-8-18.md" >}}).

## Status

$\P$-complete (Kozen <a href="#ref-1" class="reference-citation">[1]</a>).

## Remarks

A polynomial time algorithm for the problem is given in <a href="#ref-1" class="reference-citation">[1]</a>. The reduction to show hardness is from [GEN]({{< relref "./a-6-7.md" >}}).
