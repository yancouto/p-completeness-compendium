## Given

A finitely presented algebra $A = (M, \mathcal{A}, \Gamma)$ and terms $x_1, \ldots, x_n, y$. Let $[x] = \{y \in \tau \mid x \equiv y\}$. See the Uniform Word Problem for Finitely Presented Algebras, Problem A.8.18, for definitions.

## Problem

Is $[y]$ contained in the subalgebra generated by $[x_1], \ldots, [x_n]$?

## Status

P-complete problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem A.8.20.
