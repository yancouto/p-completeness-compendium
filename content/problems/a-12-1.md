## Given

A string x, a description M of a Turing machine M, and an integer t coded in unary. (To be precise, the input is the string x#M#t, where # is a delimiter character not otherwise present in the string.)

## Problem

Does M accept x within t steps?

## Status

$\P$-complete (Greenlaw, Roover and Ruzzo [3]).

## Remarks

Buss and Goldsmith show a variant of this problem is complete with respect to quasilinear time reductions for N mPl, a subclass of $\P$. See [2] for the appropriate definitions. They also show variants of Problems A.1.2 and A.7.2 are complete in this setting.
