## Given

An undirected graph $G = (V, E)$ with an ordering on the vertices and a designated vertex $v$.

## Problem

Is vertex v in the lexicographically first maximal clique of G?

## Status

P-complete problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem A.2.2.

## Remarks

Maximal cliques have important applications in parallel computation and computational complexity. The lexicographically first variant is particularly relevant to P-completeness theory as it demonstrates how seemingly small ordering constraints can push problems into the P-complete class.
