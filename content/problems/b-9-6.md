## Given

A directed graph $G = (V, E)$ with each edge labeled in unary with a capacity $c_i \geq 0$, and two distinguished vertices, source $s$ and sink $t$.

## Problem

Find a maximum flow.

## Status

Open problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem B.9.6.

## Remarks

Feather shows the problem of finding the value of the maximum flow to be in $\text{RNC}$ [103]. Karp, Upfal, and Wigderson show how to construct a maximum flow, also in $\text{RNC}$ [196]. Both problems remain in $\text{RNC}$ when capacities are polynomially bounded. Both are $\text{P}$-complete when capacities are arbitrary, see Problem A.4.4. Karpinski and Wagner show that when $G$ is given by its vertex multiplicity graph representation the Unary Network Flow Problem becomes $\text{P}$-complete [200].
