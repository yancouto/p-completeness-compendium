## Given

An encoding $\alpha$ of a Boolean circuit, inputs $x_1, \ldots, x_n$, and designated output $y$ with the additional assumption that the vertices in the circuit are numbered and listed in topological order.

## Problem

Is output $y$ of $\alpha$ true on input $x_1, \ldots, x_n$?

## Status

P-complete problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem A.1.2.

## Remarks

All of the reductions in Chapter 6 and this section preserve topological ordering, so the restrictions of all of these variants of the Circuit Value Problem to topologically ordered instances remain P-complete.
