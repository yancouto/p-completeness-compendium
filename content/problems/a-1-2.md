## Given

A Boolean circuit $\alpha$, inputs $x_1, \ldots, x_n$ and an output gate $y$, with the additional assumption that the vertices in the circuit are numbered and listed in topological order.

## Problem

Is the output $y$ of $\alpha$ true on inputs $x_1, \ldots, x_n$?

## Status

$\P$-complete under $\NC^1$ reductions [1, Theorem 6.2.1].

## Remarks

Most circuit value reductions preserve topological ordering, so the restrictions of most variants of the Circuit Value Problem to topologically ordered instances remain $\P$-complete.
