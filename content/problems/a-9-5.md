## Given

A set $S$ of $n$ points in the Euclidean plane, an integer $k$, and a designated point $p \in \R^2$.

## Problem

Is $p$ in the $k^\text{th}$ remaining convex hull that is formed by repeatedly finding and removing convex hulls from $S$?

## Status

$\P$-complete (Dessmark, Lingas, and Maheshwari [1]).

## Remarks

Chazelle shows the problem is in $\P$ [3]. The reduction is from [MLR]({{< relref "./a-8-26.md" >}}).
See [PHULL]({{< relref "./a-9-4.md" >}}) for a closely related question.
