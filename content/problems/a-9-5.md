## Given

A set $S$ of $n$ points in the Euclidean plane, an integer $k$, and a designated point $p \in \mathbb{R}^2$.

## Problem

Is $p$ in the $k$th remaining convex hull that is formed by repeatedly finding and removing convex hulls from $S$?

## Status

P-complete problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem A.9.5.
