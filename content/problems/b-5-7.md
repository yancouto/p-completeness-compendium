## Given

Two $n$-bit positive integers $a$ and $b$.

## Problem

Are $a$ and $b$ relatively prime?

## Status

Open problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem B.5.7.

## Remarks

This problem is a special case of [IntegerGCD]({{< relref "./b-5-3.md" >}}), Problem B.5.3.
