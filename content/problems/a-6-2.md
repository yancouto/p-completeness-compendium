## Given

A Horn formula $F$, that is, a conjunctive normal form (CNF) formula with each clause a disjunction of literals having at most one positive literal per clause.

## Problem

Can the empty clause $\Box$ be deduced from $F$ by unit resolution?

## Status

$\P$-complete (Dobkin, Lipton, and Reiss [1], Jones and Laaser [2]).
