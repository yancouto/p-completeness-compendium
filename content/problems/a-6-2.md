## Given

A Horn formula $F$, that is, a conjunctive normal form ($\text{CNF}$) formula with each clause a disjunction of literals having at most one positive literal per clause.

## Problem

Can the empty clause $\Box$ be deduced from $F$ by unit resolution?

## Status

P-complete problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem A.6.2.
