## Given

A group $G$.

## Problem

Find the Sylow subgroups of $G$.

## Status

Open problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem B.5.8.

## Remarks

The problem is known to be in $\P$ (Kantor [1]), however, the $\NC$ question is open even for solvable groups [2]. For a permutation group $G$, testing membership in $G$, finding the order of $G$, finding the center of $G$, and finding a composition series of $G$ are all known to be in $\NC$ [2]. Babai, Seres, and Luks present several other open questions involving group theory [2].
