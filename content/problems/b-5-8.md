## Given

A group $G$.

## Problem

Find the *Sylow subgroups* of $G$.

## Definitions

See <a href="#ref-2" class="reference-citation">[2]</a>.

## Status

Open (Babai, Seres, and Luks <a href="#ref-2" class="reference-citation">[2]</a>).

## Remarks

The problem is known to be in $\P$ (Kantor <a href="#ref-1" class="reference-citation">[1]</a>), however, the $\NC$ question is open even for solvable groups <a href="#ref-2" class="reference-citation">[2]</a>. For a permutation group $G$, testing membership in $G$, finding the order of $G$, finding the center of $G$, and finding a composition series of $G$ are all known to be in $\NC$ <a href="#ref-2" class="reference-citation">[2]</a>. Babai, Seres, and Luks present several other open questions involving group theory <a href="#ref-2" class="reference-citation">[2]</a>.
