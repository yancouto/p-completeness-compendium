## Given

A group $G$.

## Problem

Find the Sylow subgroups of $G$.

## Status

Open problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem B.5.8.

## Remarks

The problem is known to be in $\text{P}$ (Kantor [185]), however, the $\text{NC}$ question is open even for solvable groups [21]. For a permutation group $G$, testing membership in $G$, finding the order of $G$, finding the center of $G$, and finding a composition series of $G$ are all known to be in $\text{NC}$ [21]. Babai, Seres, and Luks present several other open questions involving group theory [21].
