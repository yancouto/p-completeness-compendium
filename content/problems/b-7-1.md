## Given

Input vectors $a_1, \ldots, a_n \in \mathbb{Z}^n$ that are linearly independent over $\mathbb{Q}$.

## Problem

Find a nonzero vector $x$ in the $\mathbb{Z}$-module (or "lattice") $M = \sum a_i \mathbb{Z} \subseteq \mathbb{Z}^n$ such that $\|x\| \leq 2^{(n-1)/2} \|y\|$ for all $y \in M - \\{\vec{0}\\}$, where $\|y\| = \left(\sum y_i^2\right)^{1/2}$ is the L2 norm.

## Status

Open problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem B.7.1.

## Remarks

Lenstra, Lenstra, and Lovász show that the problem is in $\P$ [1]. IntegerGCD, Problem B.5.3, is $\NC^1$ reducible to SV [2]. For additional remarks about related problems, see Bachem and Kannan [3].
