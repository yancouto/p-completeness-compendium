## Given

Let $S$ be a finite set and $F$ be the free group generated by $S$. Let $\overline{S} = \\{s, s^{-1} \mid s \in S\\}$, where $s^{-1}$ denotes the inverse of $s$. Let $\overline{S}^*$ denote the set of all finite words over $\overline{S}$. Let $e$ denote the empty word. Let $U = \\{u_1, \ldots, u_m\\}$, $V = \\{v_1, \ldots, v_p\\} \subseteq \overline{S}^*$, where $m = p \in \mathbb{N}$.

## Problem

Is $\langle U \rangle \cap \langle V \rangle \neq \langle e \rangle$?

## Status

$\P$-complete problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem A.8.15.
