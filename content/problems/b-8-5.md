## Given

A set of $n$ men and a set of $n$ women, for each person a ranking of the opposite sex according to their preference for a marriage partner, and a designated couple Alice and Bob.

## Problem

Are Alice and Bob a stable pair for the given instance of the problem? That is, is it the case that Alice and Bob are married to each other in some stable marriage?

## Status

Open (Mayr and Subramanian [1], Subramanian [2]).
