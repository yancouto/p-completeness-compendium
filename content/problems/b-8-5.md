## Given

A set of $n$ men and a set of $n$ women, for each person a ranking of the opposite sex according to their preference for a marriage partner, and a designated couple Alice and Bob.

## Problem

Are Alice and Bob a stable pair for the given instance of the problem?

## Definitions

Alice and Bob are a *stable pair* if they are married to each other in some stable marriage.

## Status

Open (Mayr and Subramanian [[1]](#1), Subramanian [[2]](#2)).

## Remarks

This problem is in $\CC$, that is, it is equivalent to [CCVP]({{< relref "./b-8-1.md" >}}).
