## Given

A bipartite graph $G = (V_1, V_2, E)$ with $|V_1| = |V_2| = n$ and a designated edge $uv$.

## Problem

(1) Is $uv$ in the matching generated by Broder's algorithm?

(2) Is $uv$ in the matching generated by Jerrum and Sinclair's algorithm?

## Definitions

Let $M_n(G)$ denote the set of perfect matchings in $G$ and $M_{n-1}(G)$ denote the set of near perfect matchings, that is, matchings of size $n-1$. Broder [[1]](#1) and Jerrum and Sinclair [[2]](#2) give probabilistic algorithms for generating elements in $M_n(G) \cup M_{n-1}(G)$. We call these algorithms *Broder's algorithm* and *Jerrum and Sinclair's algorithm*.


## Status

$\P$-complete (Teng [[3]](#3)).

## Remarks

The reductions for both these problems are from [LFMIS]({{< relref "./lfmis.md" >}}).
