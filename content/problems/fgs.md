## Given

A finitely presented algebra $A = (M, \mathcal{A}, \Gamma)$ and terms $x_1, \ldots, x_n, y$. Let $[x] = \\{y \in \tau \mid x \equiv y\\}$.

## Problem

Is $[y]$ contained in the subalgebra generated by $[x_1], \ldots, [x_n]$?

## Definitions

See [UWPFPA]({{< relref "./uwpfpa.md" >}}).

## Status

$\P$-complete (Kozen [[1]](#1)).

## Remarks

A polynomial time algorithm for the problem is given in [[1]](#1). The reduction to show hardness is from [GEN]({{< relref "./gen.md" >}}).
