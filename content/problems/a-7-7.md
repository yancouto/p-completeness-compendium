## Given

A straight-line program over alphabet $\Sigma$, $|\Sigma| \geq 1$, with operations taken from $\Phi = \Sigma \cup \\{\{\epsilon\\}, \varnothing, \cup, \cdot\\}$, and a string $x$.

## Problem

Is the set constructed by the program nonempty? See Problem A.7.6 for definition.

## Status

$\P$-complete (Goodrich [1]).

## Remarks

With $\cap$ added, SLPnonempty becomes complete for nondeterministic exponential time (Hunt, 1973).
