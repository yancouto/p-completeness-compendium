## Input

A group $G$.

## Problem

Find the *Sylow subgroups* of $G$.

## Definitions

See [[1]](#1).

## Status

Open (Babai, Seres, and Luks [[1]](#1)).

## Remarks

The problem is known to be in $\P$ (Kantor [[2]](#2)), however, the $\NC$ question is open even for solvable groups [[1]](#1). For a permutation group $G$, testing membership in $G$, finding the order of $G$, finding the center of $G$, and finding a composition series of $G$ are all known to be in $\NC$ [[1]](#1). Babai, Seres, and Luks present several other open questions involving group theory [[1]](#1).
