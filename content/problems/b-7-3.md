## Given

An $n$th degree polynomial $p \in \mathbb{Z}[x]$, and an integer $\mu$.

## Problem

For each (real or complex) root $z_i$, $1 \leq i \leq n$, of $p$, an approximation $\tilde{z}_i$ such that $|z_i - \tilde{z}_i| < 2^{-\mu}$.

## Status

Open problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem B.7.3.

## Remarks

If all the roots are real, the problem is in $\NC$ [1].
