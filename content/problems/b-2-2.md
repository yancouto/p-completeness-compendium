## Given

A linear system of inequalities $Ax \leq b$ over $\mathbb{Q}$, where each row of $A$ has at most two nonzero elements. $A$ is an $n \times d$ matrix and $b$ is an $n \times 1$ vector.

## Problem

Find a feasible solution if one exists.

## Status

Open problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem B.2.2.

## Remarks

There is a polylogarithmic algorithm that uses $n(\log n)^{O(1)}$ processors on a CREW-PRAM [248]. See also Problems A.4.1, A.4.2, A.4.3, and B.2.1.
