## Given

An undirected graph $G = (V, E)$ and a designated edge $e = uv$.

## Problem

Is $e$ in the *closure* of $G$?

## Definitions

The *closure* of a graph is the graph obtained from $G$ by repeatedly joining nonadjacent pairs of vertices $u$ and $v$ whose degree sum is at least $|V|$.

## Status

Open (Khuller [1]).

## Remarks

With the following modification, the problem becomes $\P$-complete (see Problem A.2.19): add a set of designated edges $E'$ such that only vertices whose degree sum is at least $|V|$ and whose corresponding edge is in $E'$ may be added to the closure [1].
