## Given

An undirected graph $G = (V, E)$ and a designated edge $e = \\{u, v\\}$.

## Problem

Is $e$ in the closure of $G$? That is, the graph obtained from $G$ by repeatedly joining nonadjacent pairs of vertices $u$ and $v$ whose degree sum is at least $|V|$.

## Status

Open problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem B.1.4.

## Remarks

With the following modification, the problem becomes $\text{P}$-complete (see Problem A.2.19): add a set of designated edges $E'$ such that only vertices whose degree sum is at least $|V|$ and whose corresponding edge is in $E'$ may be added to the closure [208].
