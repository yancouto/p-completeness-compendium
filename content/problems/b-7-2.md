## Given

Input vectors $a_1, a_2 \in \Z^2$ that are linearly independent over $\Q$, and a rational number $c \geq 1$.

## Problem

Find a nonzero vector $x$ such that $\|x\| \leq c\|y\|$ for all $y \in M - \\{\vec{0}\\}$.

## Status

Open (von zur Gathen <a href="#ref-1" class="reference-citation">[1]</a>).
