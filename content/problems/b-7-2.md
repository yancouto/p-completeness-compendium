## Given

Input vectors $a_1, a_2 \in \mathbb{Z}^2$ that are linearly independent over $\mathbb{Q}$, and a rational number $c \geq 1$.

## Problem

Find a nonzero vector $x$ such that $\|x\| \leq c\|y\|$ for all $y \in M - \\{\vec{0}\\}$.

## Status

Open problem from Greenlaw et al. (1995), Problem B.7.2.

## Remarks

[IntegerGCD]({{< relref "./b-5-3.md" >}}), Problem B.5.3, is $\NC^1$ reducible to SV2 [1]. For additional remarks about related problems, see Bachem and Kannan [2].
