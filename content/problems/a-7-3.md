## Given

A context-free grammar $G = (N, T, P, S)$.

## Problem

Is $L(G)$ infinite?

## Status

$\P$-complete (Goldschlager [[1]](#1), Jones and Laaser [[2]](#2)).

## Remarks

The reduction to show hardness is from [MCVP]({{< relref "./a-1-3.md" >}}), a variant of [CFGempty]({{< relref "./a-7-2.md" >}}).
