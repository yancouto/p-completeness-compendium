## Given

A context-free grammar $G = (N, T, P, S)$.

## Problem

Is $L(G)$ empty?

## Status

$\P$-complete (Jones and Laaser [2], Goldschlager [1]).

## Remarks

Note, this reduction and the one for CFGinf, Problem A.7.3, have no $\epsilon$-productions yet remain complete. The original proof of Jones and Laaser reduced Generability, Problem A.6.7, to CFGempty. Their proof used the reduction for CFGmem, Problem A.7.1, and instead checked if $L(G)$ is empty [2].
