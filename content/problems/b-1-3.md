## Given

An undirected graph $G = (V, E)$ with positive integer weights on the edges.

## Problem

Find a matching of maximum weight.

## Definitions

A matching is a subset of edges that share no endpoints.

## Status

Open (Karp, Upfal, and Wigderson [[1]](#1)).

## Remarks

EWM is in $\mathsf{RNC}$ if all weights are polynomially bounded [[1]](#1). Even in this restricted case, the problem is not known to be in $\NC$. Define $\text{rank}(e)$ to be the position of edge $e$'s weight in the sorted list of edge weights, where the sorting is done in increasing order. [LFMM]({{< relref "./b-8-2.md" >}}) (and hence all of $\CC$), is $\NC$ reducible to EWM, by assigning weight $2^{\text{rank}(e)}$ to edge $e$.
