## Given

An undirected graph $G = (V, E)$ with $\Delta$ equal to the maximum degree of any vertex in $V$.

## Problem

Find an edge coloring of $G$ that uses less than or equal to $\Delta + 1$ colors. An edge coloring is an assignment of colors to the edges such that no incident edges receive the same color.

## Status

Open (Karloﬀ and Shmoys [1]).

## Remarks

An $\NC$ algorithm is known for polylogarithmic $\Delta$ [1]. Karloff and Shmoys give an $\mathsf{RNC}$ algorithm on the COMMON CRCW-PRAM that uses $\Delta + 20 \cdot \Delta^{1/2 + \epsilon}$ colors with running time $(\log |V|)^{O(1)}$ and processors $|V|^{O(1)}$, where the running time is for a fixed $\epsilon > 0$ and the processor bound is independent of $\epsilon$. Also see Problems A.2.6 and B.1.2.
